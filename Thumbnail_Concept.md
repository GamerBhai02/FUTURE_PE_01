## 2. Visual Description

**Main idea: Human vs AI tension**

- **Left side (Human):**
  - You sitting at a desk
  - Messy desk, phone in hand
  - Confused / tired / skeptical expression
  - Warm, natural lighting
- **Right side (AI):**
  - Laptop or phone screen showing ChatGPT interface
  - Clean, glowing green or white UI
  - Checklist or timeline visible on screen
  - Cool, futuristic lighting
- **Center element:**
  - A bold arrow or divider pointing from ChatGPT → You
  - Suggests “control” or “decision flow”
- **Text placement:**
  - Big, bold text at the top or center
  - High contrast (white or yellow text with dark outline)
  - No clutter—only ONE short text line

This instantly communicates:

- AI is in charge
- A real human is reacting
- Something unusual happened
